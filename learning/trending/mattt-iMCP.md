**Added:** 2026-02-01 02:25

## What is iMCP?

macOS app by mattt that connects your digital life to AI via MCP:
- **Calendar** - Events, recurrence, alarms
- **Messages** - Message history
- **Contacts** - Search by name/phone/email
- **Location** - Current location, coordinates
- **Maps** - Place search, directions
- **Reminders** - View/create with due dates
- **Weather** - Current conditions

```
macOS Apps → iMCP (MCP Server) → AI Client (Claude Desktop)
```
